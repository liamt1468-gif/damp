Frequently Asked Questions

How long does verification take? +
Most verifications are processed within 24-48 hours. Emirates ID verification is typically instant, while police clearance checks may take 2-3 business days depending on the complexity of the request.
Is my data secure? +
Yes, absolutely. We use advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your personal information. All data is stored securely and handled in compliance with UAE data protection regulations.
What documents do I need? +
Requirements vary by service. For Emirates ID verification, you'll need your Emirates ID number. For police clearance, you'll need a valid passport and UAE residence visa. For bank verification, you'll need your account details and authorization.
Can I track my application? +
Yes! Each application receives a unique tracking ID. You can use this ID to check the status of your verification request at any time. You'll also receive email notifications for important updates.
What are the fees? +
Fees vary depending on the type of verification. Emirates ID verification: AED 50, Police clearance certificate: AED 100, Bank verification: AED 75. All fees are payable online through secure payment gateways.
