Overview

Dubai Police Force was established in early June 1956, with its first headquarters located at Naif Fort, where it remained until 1973. The Force later moved to its current headquarters in the Al Twar North area, while Naif Fort was converted into one of Dubai Police's active stations.

Today, Dubai Police stand among the most advanced law enforcement agencies in the world. The General Command is dedicated to safeguarding the security, safety, and stability of the Emirate of Dubai. Its mission goes beyond maintaining public order; it also encompasses delivering exceptional services to every resident and visitor across the emirate.

Our Mission

To provide world-class policing services that ensure the safety and security of all residents and visitors in Dubai, while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and community engagement.

With state-of-the-art technology, highly trained personnel, and a commitment to innovation, Dubai Police continues to set new benchmarks in law enforcement excellence. Our verification services are part of this commitment to transparency, security, and efficient public service delivery.
